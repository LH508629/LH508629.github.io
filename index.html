Recipe #1: Fried Chicken

  • Time: 7-8 mins on each side.
  • Serves: 8oz = 8serviings
Ingredients Quantity
seasoning 1/2 cup
chicken 12 pieces
flower 4 cups

Step 1: Rinse the chicken and prep your batter.

Step 2: Dip the chicken one at a time in the batter then pre heat the oil.

Step 3: Drop the chicken in the oil and fry until they're golden brown and float to the top.

Recipe #2: Soul Style-Spaghetti

  • Time: 30-45 mins
  • Serves: 8+ servings
Ingredients Quantity
spaghetti 3 boxes
meat 4 lbs
cheese 1 lbs
sauce 2 cups

Step 1: Clean meat while boiling pasta and preheating sauce.

Step 2: Cook meat welldone and season as well, then mix meat and sauce together.

Step 3: Place pasta in the pane pour the mixed sauce on top then cover the top with cheese and bake for 15mins.

Recipe #3: Soul Style-Mac N Cheese Casserole

  • Time: 25-35 mins
  • Serves: 8+ servings
Ingredients Quantity
macaroni 6 cups
milk 8 cups
cheese 1 lbs

Step 1: Boil milk then place cheese begin to stir and add a few tbl spoons of flower while boiling macaroni.

Step 2: When done mix cheese sauce and macaroni together.

Step 3: Pour Mac N Cheese into pan then cover the to with cheese and bake for 8-10mins.
